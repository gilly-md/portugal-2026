The three places — to help you choose

A quick feel for each, so the questions below (especially the last few) are easy to answer.

Porto

A soulful riverside city — port-wine lodges, blue-tiled façades and the Douro waterfront at Ribeira & Gaia. Lively, walkable, wonderful food. Romance with city atmosphere.

Your arrival side · ~3h from Lisbon

The Douro Valley

Portugal's wine country — terraced vineyards tumbling to the river, quintas with infinity pools and slow tastings. Serene, scenic and indulgent; the quiet heart of the trip.

~1–1.5h inland from Porto · car or transfers

Lisbon

The capital you fly in and out of — hilltop viewpoints, trams, fado and the tiled lanes of Alfama, with big-city buzz. Romantic but busier; a short bookend, or a fuller stop if you want city energy.

In & out airport · kept light unless you want more
