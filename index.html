Good to know

Monverde is a Vinho Verde wine estate — your seclusion-and-winery base. The Douro's dramatic river-gorge views come on a day-trip: Amarante sits ~45 min from both Porto and the Douro Valley, so one Douro-gorge day (river cruise + a viewpoint + a port-quinta tasting) gives you the gorge and the port wine without moving hotels.
